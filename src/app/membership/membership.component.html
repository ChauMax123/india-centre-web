Membership Policies

At India Centre Latvia, we aim to build a welcoming community that celebrates Indian culture and fosters cultural exchange. Through our variety of programs, such as yoga, cultural festivals, and group excursions, we create a space for wellness, relaxation, and connection.

To maintain a supportive and respectful environment, we have established membership policies that guide facility use, membership benefits, and the overall member experience. Below are the key aspects of our policies:

    General Policies
    • Membership is non-transferable and non-refundable.
    • Benefits apply only to active members.
    Family Membership Policies
    • Children above 18 years are not included in the family membership and must enroll separately.
    • No holds are allowed on annual memberships.
    Annual Membership Policies
    • Annual memberships cannot be canceled unless based on disability or relocation with appropriate proof.
    • Cancellation due to disability requires a doctor's certificate.
    Access Policy & Day Pass Rates
    • Membership grants access to all areas of the facility except staff areas.
    • Non-members can purchase a day pass for $10 for temporary access.
    • $10 fee for lost cards for full access members.
    Cancellation & Hold Policy
    • Members can cancel or hold their membership by providing a signed notice or submitting a cancellation/hold form.
    • Cancellation requests will be processed based on terms like notice dates, with dues continuing to accrue until the cancellation is effective.
    • The hold period can be maintained for a maximum of 3 months per year.
    • Rejoining after cancellation requires a new enrollment fee and dues at the current rate.

    For clarifications on policies, or more information, please email info@Indiacc.org
