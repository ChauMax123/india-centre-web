Welcome to the India Centre Latvia

Connecting the Indian community and our Latvian friends in a celebration of culture, joy, and unity.


Experience the Essence of India


At India Centre Latvia, we bring the vibrant essence of India to the heart of Latvia, uniting the Indian community and our Latvian friends in a celebration of culture, joy, and togetherness. Our mission is to create a welcoming space that fosters a sense of belonging, offers enriching cultural experiences, and builds meaningful connections. Whether you’re here to reconnect with your roots, experience the lively spirit of Indian festivals, or explore our rich cultural heritage, India Centre Latvia is truly your home away from home.

About Us


India Centre Latvia was established in 2023 to create a vibrant space for the Indian community in Latvia. Our journey began with a shared vision of uniting people through cultural events and fostering friendships. We aim to preserve and promote Indian traditions while also integrating with the local culture. A world where cultures unite to create stronger communities, understanding, and mutual respect.
