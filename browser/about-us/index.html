About IC

Welcome to India Centre, where we celebrate Indian culture and bring together the community through cultural, social, and recreational programs.

Our Story

India Centre Latvia was established in 2023 to create a vibrant space for the Indian community in Latvia. Our journey began with a shared vision of uniting people through cultural events and fostering friendships. We aim to preserve and promote Indian traditions while also integrating with the local culture.

Mission & Vision

To celebrate, promote, and nurture Indian culture in Latvia, and foster unity within the community through cultural exchange and understanding. A world where cultures unite to create stronger communities, understanding, and mutual respect.

Programs and Activities

At India Centre Latvia, we celebrate the richness of Indian culture through a variety of programs and activities. Our cultural festivals bring together the community with vibrant performances and traditional food, offering a taste of India’s festive spirit. Additionally, we host recreational activities such as yoga sessions, sports meets, and group excursions, creating a space for relaxation, wellness, and social bonding.
